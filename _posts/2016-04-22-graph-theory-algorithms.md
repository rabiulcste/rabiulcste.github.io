#### Why Graph Theory

Graph theory is an important topic in computer science algorithm arena. To be a good programmer you need to know a couple of graph theory algorithms. Top sites like google, facebook or others, where searching is needed, you need to conduct with graph theory. Here, I just wrote code of different popular graph theory algorithms. If you want to know theoretical details or pseudo-code you may love to visit [Shafayet Vai’s Blog](http://www.shafaetsplanet.com/planetcoding/). He explained graph theory in a very good way. I think he is the best tutorial maker in Bengali language for algorithms. You should read CLRS book to know details.
